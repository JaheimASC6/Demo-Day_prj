About us
In 2018, about 69% of students took out student loans , graduating with an average debt balance of $29,800, according to Student Loan Hero. Having this large of a debt can cause lots of stress and worry for students who arent coming out immediately with a job. Our goal in this is to create an easy way for willing students to make money to lower their total expenses and come out with less debt.
