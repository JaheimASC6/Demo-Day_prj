Here are a couple of websites that give good scholarship options.

collegescholarships.com -A site especially made for students across a wide time of years. This website allows you to find scholarships as early as your first year in highschool to beyond your 3rd year of grad school. It is also easy to use and gives recourses to help you on your way to getting a good scholarship.

scholarships.com -This is another site similar to collegescholar.com that has a wider variety of options to schoose from. It lists multiple options to select that narrow down the search for a scholarship. It also gives examples of how to keep everything orderly sych as a calender.

niche.com/scholarships-Niche allows users to complete scholarships online and submit them at the same time. You can create an account and view all the scholarships you have acceplted and completed currently. You can also select apply for scholarship money that supports your major.

unigo.com/scholarships Unigo gives special deals that students can participate in for a larger sum of money. It also has articles that give help on how to pay and be ready for college.

fastweb.com/scholarships Fastweb gives scholarships as well as other student benefits for joining their website. For example, students who use this site will get special deals with Dell.Fastweb also offers part-time jobs to those looking for one.
